Welcome to the Innovative Educators technical documentation site. Below are the 2 types of itegrations we currently offer.

Make authentication seamless for learners and maintain data accuracy by automating learner account creation via **SAML** or **CAS** protocols.

## [Data Integrations](./data-integrations/introduction)
